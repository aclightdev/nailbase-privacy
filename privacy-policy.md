# Privacy Policy for NailBase

**Last updated:** July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NailBase ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Overview

NailBase is an offline-first application designed for nail masters and salons to manage client records, visits, services and income. **All data you enter into NailBase is stored locally on your device only**, unless you explicitly enable the optional Google Drive sync feature described in Section 6. We do not operate any server, and we do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data.

## 2. Information We Collect

NailBase stores the following information **locally on your device**:

- **Client information:** names, phone numbers, photos and referral relationships that you manually enter.
- **Visit and service records:** dates, services performed, durations and payment amounts that you manually enter.
- **Master/salon profile:** your name, phone, address, working hours and business card design (logo, colors, font) that you add.
- **App preferences:** selected language, currency, vibration/sound settings, and app lock preference, stored on your device.

None of this information is sent to us, to any server, or to any third party, except your own Google Drive account if you explicitly enable the optional sync feature (Section 6). We have no ability to view, access, or recover this data.

## 3. Permissions We Use

NailBase requests the following device permissions, used solely for the purposes described:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Camera** | To photograph clients and scan QR codes for the referral and salon-report features. Photos and scans are processed and stored locally on your device only. |
| **Biometric / Device credential** | To provide an optional app lock feature, if you choose to enable it in Settings. When biometric unlock is available and used, it is handled entirely by the Android operating system; NailBase never accesses or stores your fingerprint or face data. The app lock PIN itself is stored only as a salted hash on your device, never in plain text. |
| **Contacts (picker only)** | When you choose to import a contact's name and phone number into a client record, NailBase uses the Android system contact picker. NailBase does not request broad access to your contact list and cannot read your contacts outside of the specific contact you choose to import. |
| **Vibration** | For tap/success/error haptic feedback - purely cosmetic, no data involved. |

## 4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in the app's private storage area on your device, which is not accessible to other apps. If you enable the optional App Lock feature, access to the app requires a PIN code (and, on supported devices, biometric authentication as a faster alternative) before the app can be opened.

## 5. Backup and Data Export

NailBase includes an optional manual backup feature that creates a ZIP file containing your data (database and client/business-card photos) on your device. **You control where this file goes** - the App uses the standard Android share function, and you choose the destination (for example, your own cloud storage or messaging app). We do not have access to this file at any point, and the App itself does not upload it anywhere automatically.

## 6. Optional Google Drive Sync

NailBase offers an additional, **optional** backup method: automatic synchronization to your own Google Drive account. This feature is **off by default** - nothing is sent anywhere unless you explicitly turn it on in Settings and sign in with your Google account.

If you choose to enable it:

- **What is synced:** your client and visit records, service history, and client photos - the same data described in Section 2.
- **Where it goes:** a private, app-specific storage area on **your own** Google Drive account, called "appDataFolder". This area is not visible in your regular Google Drive file list and cannot be accessed by us, by NailBase's developer, or by any other app - only by NailBase itself, using your Google authorization. It is not our server; we do not have access to it.
- **When it happens:** automatically in the background, only after a new client, visit, or photo is saved, and only while the feature is enabled. If a device has no internet connection at that moment, the sync is queued and completes once a connection is available.
- **How to turn it off:** open Settings → "Sync with Google Drive" and switch it off at any time. Once disabled, no further data is sent, and you can revoke NailBase's access to your Google account entirely at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

This feature exists to help you avoid losing data if your device is lost, damaged, or replaced. Turning it on does not change how your data is stored locally - it remains in your device's private storage as described in Section 4, in addition to being copied to your own Google Drive.

## 7. Third-Party Services

NailBase does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. QR code scanning (referral and salon-report features) uses on-device machine learning (Google ML Kit); this processing happens entirely on your device and does not send images or data to Google or any other party.

## 8. In-App Purchases

NailBase offers an optional one-time "Pro" purchase through Google Play Billing to unlock additional features. Purchase processing is handled entirely by Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment information. Please refer to [Google Play's own Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information on how Google handles payment data.

## 9. Your Control Over Your Data

Because all data is stored only on your device:

- You can delete any client, visit, or service record at any time from within the App.
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the App.
- You are solely responsible for any backup files you create and share; deleting those files is your responsibility as well.

## 10. Children's Privacy

NailBase is a business tool intended for use by nail-service professionals and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
